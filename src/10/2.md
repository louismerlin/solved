# GLASSHEADS, pt. 3 Challenge #2 - Backwards

This challenge can be found when the team figures-out that they can walk backwards in the first puzzle room.

## The Setup

> You've intercepted some audio. Can you figure out what it says? 

We get an mp3 file.

## The Sounds

The file sounds like a backwards piece of audio, which the title of the challenge heavily implies too.

In order to get the flag, I used [audacity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Audacity_(audio_editor)).
Open the mp3, select the track, then do Effect > Special > Reverse and play the audio.
The key will be spelled out to you.
