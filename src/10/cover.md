# GLASSHEADS, pt. 3 Cover Challenge

This challenge can be found on the cover of issue 10. Spot the symbols on the bottom right corner.

## The digits

These look like values from a [seven-segment display](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seven-segment_display) but they do not make any sense.
Some of the digits are almost recognizable, which led me to the realization that they were mirrored upside-down and left-to-right.

After mirroring a photo of the digits, we can see these are not just digits but hexadecimal.
Decoding the hex, we get the URL that leads us to the flag.
